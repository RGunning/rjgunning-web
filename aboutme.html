About Me


I am finishing up a PhD studying the relationship between epigenetic factors and genetic splicing as part of a BBSRC DTP PhD project at the University of Cambridge. I am also a founding member of the History To The Public Project, currently serving as Creative Director.

I serve as webmaster for a number of websites:

  • History To The Public
  • Clare College Cambridge MCR
  • Clare College Boat Club
  • Clare College Cambridge Clareity

For both History To The Public and Clare College Cambridge MCR, I designed the website layout. I have also completed an AngularJS booking website for Clare College Cambridge's punts.

In my freetime, I work as a freelance programmer for website Design and Forex Metatrader EA development. Contact me for any jobs you would like done.
